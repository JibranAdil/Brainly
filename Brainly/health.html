Sleep is one of the most important factors when it comes to brain health. Getting an adequate amount of sleep ensures that your brain is able to recover for the next day as well as learn and strengthen synaptic connections you experienced when awake.

Exercise is vital for the brain as it can improve learning, problem-solving, emotional balance and reduce anxiety or depression.

Getting sunlight outside during the early part of the day as well as later in the day is important in helping your brain regulate your sleep-wake cycle.
